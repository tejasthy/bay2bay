Bay2Bay Foundation

Bay2Bay Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization that provides free personalized tutoring services for K-12 students in the Bay Area. It offers highly interactive instructional modules that center on real-world application. We plan to launch during the 2024-25 academic school year. Being a nonprofit organization, Bay2Bay Education is made possible by volunteers who assist in administrative work, the interpretation of California State Education Standards, the creation of curriculum, and other aspects of the nonprofit.

Questions? Contact us @ info@bay2bay.org
